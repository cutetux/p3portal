This document describes the names, pseudonyms, and domain names associated
with the P3 Portal project and their legal status.  It follows the spirit
of the Apache License 2.0 §6 trademark reservation.

## 1  Word Mark – "P3 Portal" (planned)

**"P3 Portal"** is the product name of this software, used by the author
since initial publication.

- **Current legal status:** The author claims protection under §4 Nr. 2
  MarkenG (German Trade Mark Act) based on use in commerce (*Verkehrsgeltung
  durch Benutzung*).  A formal DPMA (German Patent and Trade Mark Office)
  registration for the word mark "P3 Portal" is **planned** but **not yet
  filed** as of this writing.

> **Disclaimer:** No registered trade mark exists for "P3 Portal" at the
> time of this document.  The DPMA application is a future administrative
> task and does not create retroactive rights.  This notice documents intent
> and existing use-based protection only.

### Usage rules

This license (AGPLv3 for Core, LICENSE-PLUS for Plus) **does not grant**
permission to use the name "P3 Portal" in any of the following ways:

- As the name or part of the name of a derivative work or fork.
- As a product or service identifier in a manner that could suggest the
  product is the official P3 Portal or is endorsed by the author.
- In domain names, package names, or organisation names that could create
  confusion with the original project.

**Permitted use:** You may use the name "P3 Portal" in truthful,
non-misleading references to this software (e.g. "based on P3 Portal",
"fork of P3 Portal") provided that you clearly identify your work as a
distinct, unendorsed derivative.

## 2  Author Pseudonym – "rootq"

**"rootq"** is the published pseudonym of the sole author of P3 Portal,
protected under §13 UrhG (German Copyright Act – right to authorship).

- **Legal basis:** The right of an author to be identified by a pseudonym
  is a non-transferable moral right under §13 UrhG.  This is **not** a
  registered or unregistered trade mark.  No trade-mark rights are claimed
  for "rootq".

> **Clarity note:** Using "rootq" as a trade mark would require a separate
> registration that does not exist.  This document therefore expressly
> disclaims any trade-mark claim to "rootq" and relies solely on the
> author's §13 UrhG right to authorship identification.

### Usage rules

- Derivative works must **not** use the pseudonym "rootq" as a project or
  product identifier in a manner that could suggest endorsement by the
  author.
- You may use "rootq" in truthful attribution notices (e.g. "originally
  authored by rootq").
- The **real name** of the author behind the pseudonym is not disclosed in
  this repository.  Any attempt to link the pseudonym to a real person in a
  public forum without the author's consent may constitute a violation of
  §12 BGB (right to one's name) and §13 UrhG.

## 4  Confusion and Endorsement Clause

Combining the above, the following rules apply to all derivative works,
forks, redistributions (of Core under AGPLv3), and any other use of the
P3 Portal source code:

1. **"P3 Portal" name confusion:** Derivative works must not use names
   that could create confusion with "P3 Portal" or with the domains
   `p3portal.org` / `rootq.de`.

2. **"rootq" pseudonym endorsement:** Derivative works must not use the
   author pseudonym "rootq" as a project or product identifier in a
   manner that could suggest endorsement by the author.
