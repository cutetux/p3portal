# My Permissions

This page shows exactly what you are allowed to do in the portal.

## Sections
- **Proxmox roles** – roles assigned to your Proxmox user
- **Portal permissions** – special flags granted by an admin (e.g. manage_nodes)
- **Role presets** – bundled permission sets applied to your account
- **Node assignments** – Proxmox nodes you can access and what actions are allowed
- **Groups** – teams you belong to (inherited permissions)

## How permissions work
Proxmox role presets grant access to VM/LXC actions. Portal permissions control access to admin features. Both can be assigned directly or via groups.
